About Me

I have been active in the IT world since 1999 when I took my first internship with the McCreary County School District and assisted in creation of Microsoft Exchange 5.5 servers and Proxies for the district. Since then I have had a wonderful career in IT that has spanned everything from Service Desks, Knowledge Management, End User Services Management, Software Engineering, and Project Management. Having experienced so much of the IT ecosystem, I have found that cybersecurity is not only an essential component of our lives, but also one of the foundational principles we must adhere to in the modern world. Red Teaming may get all the glory in media, but it is the policies, audits, and compliances that really make the field work. I'm using my experience as a project manager and business analyst to step into the world of Governance, Risk, and Compliance (GRC).
